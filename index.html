ABOUT DONATIONS

We provide financial aid, textbooks and computers for children with precarious material situations, so they can afford to go to school and learn like a child should have the chance to in the 21th century.

The background of each family that is offered a help through EDUX is previously checked and the evolution of the children is constantly monitored. In that way we can be sure that we serve the right purpose. If you would like to support our cause and give an opportunity to succeed in life to a child, do not hesitate to help through a donation.

You can donate money, books or computers.

ABOUT US

Irina Spac
MAIN FOUNDER

EDUX is a non-profit organization with the only goal of helping children from eastern Europe to have a chance to an educational program.

The organization was established in 2015 by two warm hearted young students from Copenhagen. So that no matter the country, the future generations can make a difference in the world and this can happen only through education.

We are living in the century of speed where aspects like education are often taken for granted, but there are children out there for which this normal thing is a luxury. Children from rural areas with disorganized families or with one of the parents no longer alive.

With more than 40% of the teenagers (aged 15) from some eastern European countries being functionally illiterate, there is a need for constructively actions.

Through donations of modest sums of money, textbooks and computers that we no longer need, we can change the life of a child and support a future adult that can bring many contributions to the society.
